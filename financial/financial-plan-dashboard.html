Drawdown rules

Normal operation

  • Spend from BNZ savings (B1 float)
  • Every 6 months: if float < $30K, top up to ~$50K from maturing TD; roll TD
  • Every 12 months (or when markets up): move ~$80K B2 → B1 TDs

Refill triggers

  • B2 → B1: when B1 TDs < $150K, transfer $125K (only if B2 not down >15% from peak)
  • B3 → B2: when B2 < $600K, restore to $1M (only if B3 not down >20% from peak)

Market downturn protocol

  • <15% drop: continue normal operation
  • 15–30% drop: pause B2→B1 top-ups; draw down B1 float; wait for recovery
  • >30% drop: live on B1 only; do not sell fund units; reassess after 12 months
